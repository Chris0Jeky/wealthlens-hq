# Emails To Send

Last updated: 2026-05-14

Draft outreach emails here before sending. Always check `emails-sent.md` for prior contact history before sending.

**Outreach rules:**
- Lead with what you've already built, not what you want to build.
- Be professional, specific, and value-offering.
- Include a link to something already built whenever possible.
- Never send without checking prior contact history first.
- Do not over-promise.
