# Content Calendar

Last updated: 2026-05-14

Weekly content plan for WealthLens and Chris's public voice. Run everything from personal accounts initially.

## Content Pillars

1. **Public-interest engineering** (how engineering makes inequality visible)
2. **Inequality by the numbers** (data-driven charts and insights) — 40% of content
3. **Widening participation** (education access, social mobility, personal stories)
4. **Power and systems** (how wealth concentration works structurally)

## Content Mix

- 50% explanatory (data + charts)
- 20% build-in-public (progress updates, technical decisions)
- 20% personal-professional story (WP work, career journey, motivation)
- 10% direct opinion (carefully sourced)

## Rules

- No external links in LinkedIn post body (kills reach); put links in first comment.
- LinkedIn document carousels outperform all other formats (~596% more engagement).
- Engage with every comment within 30 minutes of posting.
- One number, one chart, one sentence per social post; save complexity for the website.
- Use hashtags sparingly: #WealthInequality #OpenData #EconomicJustice #WideningParticipation
- Never make unsourced claims.
- Do not repost Gary's clips without adding your own data angle.
