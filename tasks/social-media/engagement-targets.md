# Engagement Targets

Last updated: 2026-05-14

Accounts, people, and conversations to engage with regularly.

## Rules

- Add value before linking.
- Be specific — reference data, not vibes.
- Ask good questions.
- Do not turn every reply into a pitch.
- Reply into big accounts (highest-ROI activity at 0-1K followers).
- Engage same-day when IFS, Resolution Foundation, JRF, or NEF drop reports.
- Quote-tweet Gary's Economics with independent data analysis, not reposting.
