# Principles

## Operating Principles

- Data first, opinion second.
- Open source always.
- Accessible by default.
- Independent and non-partisan.
- Ship something real before building process around it.
- Make the work visible enough that the right people can find it.
- Prefer public artefacts: charts, data, notebooks, methods notes, posts, and demos.

## Decision Rule

When uncertain, optimise in this order:

1. Ship something real.
2. Make it visible.
3. Connect with the right people.
