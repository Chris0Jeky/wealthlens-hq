# Content Strategy

Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Working Cadence

- Monday: flagship post, blog, or chart page.
- Tuesday: short chart take on X/Bluesky.
- Wednesday: LinkedIn post or carousel.
- Thursday: short video or behind-the-scenes build note.
- Friday: reflection, response, or learning note.

## Content Formats

- Chart explainer.
- Bad metric / better metric.
- Notebook to narrative.
- Build-in-public update.
- Methodology in plain English.
- Personal story tied to evidence.
