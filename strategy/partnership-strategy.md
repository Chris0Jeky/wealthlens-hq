# Partnership Strategy

Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Partnership Offer

WealthLens can give organisations source-backed, embeddable, public-friendly charts that help people understand UK wealth inequality without having to rebuild data infrastructure.

## Good Partner Fit

- Organisations that already cite inequality statistics.
- Organisations that need reusable charts or explainers.
- Researchers and journalists who need public-facing evidence artefacts.
- Educators who need accessible material.

## First Outreach Rule

Lead with a specific useful artefact, not a general project pitch.
