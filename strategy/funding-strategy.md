# Funding Strategy

Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Phase 1

No funding required beyond basic hosting and tooling. Focus on shipping credible public artefacts.

## Phase 2

Cover small operating costs transparently if usage grows.

## Phase 3

Explore grants, fiscal sponsorship, CIC structure, donations, or partnerships only after there is evidence of public value.

## Notes

Any funding path must preserve independence, methodological credibility, and open-source access.
