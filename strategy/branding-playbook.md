# Branding Playbook

Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Voice

- Confident but not arrogant.
- Data-driven and accessible.
- Personal where useful.
- Non-partisan.
- Specific rather than abstract.

## Core Positioning

Engineer making unequal systems legible.

## Content Rule

One chart, one mechanism, one question.

## Platforms

- LinkedIn for authority and professional credibility.
- X and Bluesky for conversation and discovery.
- Instagram/TikTok for short visual explanations and personal reach.
- Own-domain blog or newsletter for durable archives.
