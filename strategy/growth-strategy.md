# Growth Strategy

Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Growth Loops

- Publish a chart page.
- Cut it into platform-native posts.
- Invite critique and corrections.
- Improve methodology.
- Make the chart embeddable.
- Use the artefact in outreach.

## Early Signals

- Genuine replies from people outside the existing network.
- Saves and shares from researchers, journalists, campaigners, or educators.
- GitHub issues or pull requests from contributors.
- Requests to reuse charts.
