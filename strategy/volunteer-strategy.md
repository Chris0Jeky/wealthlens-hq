# Volunteer Strategy

Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Starting Principle

Do not build heavy community infrastructure before there are active contributors. Make the first contribution path obvious and low-friction.

## Contributor Roles

- Engineering
- Data and research
- Design and UX
- Content and communications
- Strategy and partnerships
- Amplification and feedback

## Needed Assets

- Clear `CONTRIBUTING.md`.
- Good first issues.
- Plain-language task board.
- Recognition through the contributors and thank-yous logs (private HQ repo: `../hq-private/projects/wealthlens/people/`; publish recognition via the public site/README when contributors consent).
