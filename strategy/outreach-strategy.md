# Outreach Strategy

Last updated: 2026-06-13

## Rule

Do not ask for favours before showing useful work. Outreach should offer value: a chart, dataset, prototype, methodology note, or contributor opportunity.

## Before Contacting Anyone

Contact lists and email logs live in the private HQ repo (`../hq-private/projects/wealthlens/outreach/`):

- Check `contacts.md`.
- Check `emails-sent.md`.
- Check whether there is already a relevant draft in `emails-to-send.md`.

## Target Groups

- Tax Justice UK
- Patriotic Millionaires UK
- The Equality Trust
- Resolution Foundation
- Institute for Fiscal Studies
- Data journalists
- Widening participation and social mobility organisations
