Deliverable 04 — Brand mark

WealthLens UK — Lens & Lorenz

The mark is a magnifying lens framing a Lorenz curve — the canonical inequality shape. The dashed line is equality. The red curve is reality. The mark literally shows the gap. Works at favicon scale and in single colour. Pairs with Newsreader for the wordmark.

User pick 01 · Lens & Lorenz selected as the primary mark. Variations 02–04 retained as future options.
