# Mission

WealthLens UK makes the data behind wealth inequality visible, interactive, and impossible to ignore so that everyone can see the system clearly and push for better decisions.

## Working Positioning

The UK has strong public data on wealth, housing, income, inheritance, capital gains, and regional inequality. Much of it is buried in spreadsheets, PDFs, and specialist portals. WealthLens turns that evidence into source-backed, accessible, shareable public tools.

## Boundaries

- WealthLens is a data transparency project, not a political party.
- WealthLens should show evidence clearly and cite sources directly.
- WealthLens should avoid claims that cannot be audited.
