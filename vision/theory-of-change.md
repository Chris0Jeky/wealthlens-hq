# Theory Of Change

## Working Chain

Visibility leads to understanding. Understanding leads to better public conversation. Better public conversation creates pressure for better decisions, research, journalism, education, and policy.

## Mechanism

1. Public data is collected and normalised.
2. Methodology is documented in plain English.
3. Charts are made mobile-first, accessible, shareable, and embeddable.
4. Journalists, educators, campaigners, researchers, and citizens reuse the artefacts.
5. Better evidence becomes easier to circulate than vague claims.

## Product Implication

Prioritise permanent chart pages, downloads, embeds, and methodology notes over a large all-purpose dashboard.
