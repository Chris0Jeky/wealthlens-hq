# Horizon

## 1 Year

- A public WealthLens site with a small library of source-backed chart pages.
- Reproducible data pipelines for the first official datasets.
- A clear contributor path and a small group of active collaborators.
- A recognisable public voice around inequality data transparency.

## 3 Years

- A reusable open-source infrastructure layer for UK inequality charts.
- Partnerships with charities, campaign groups, educators, journalists, or research organisations.
- Regularly refreshed datasets and public methodology notes.

## 5 Years

- WealthLens becomes trusted public infrastructure for people trying to understand UK wealth concentration, housing affordability, inheritance, taxation, and regional opportunity.
