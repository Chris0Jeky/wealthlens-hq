# Reading Queue

Last updated: 2026-05-14

Prioritised reading list for WealthLens and public-interest engineering. Extracted from all research documents.

## Tier 1: Essential (Read First)

### Books
- [ ] *A Brief History of Equality* — Thomas Piketty (2022). Cover-to-cover. Start here. [priority: Week 1]
- [ ] *Inequality: What Can Be Done?* — Anthony Atkinson (2015). "Single most useful book." List 15 proposals and assess each against UK policy. [priority: Week 3-4]
- [ ] *The Trading Game* — Gary Stevenson (Penguin). Order immediately. Primary inspiration context. [priority: Week 1]
- [ ] *Capital in the Twenty-First Century* — Thomas Piketty (2014). Read chapters 1-3 at minimum; free PDF summary at wir2022.wid.world. [priority: Weeks 2-6]
- [ ] *Global Inequality* — Branko Milanovic (2016). Elephant curve. [priority: Weeks 3-4]
- [ ] *The Hidden Wealth of Nations* — Gabriel Zucman (2015). Offshore wealth. [priority: Weeks 5-6]
- [ ] *Rentier Capitalism* — Brett Christophers (2020). Most important UK-applied political economist. [priority: Week 5]
- [ ] *The Spirit Level* — Wilkinson & Pickett (2009). Read critically alongside Snowdon's critique. [priority: Week 6]

### Reports
- [ ] Resolution Foundation *Wealth Check* (July 2024) [priority: Week 2]
- [ ] Resolution Foundation *Inequality Control* (November 2024) [priority: Week 2]
- [ ] Resolution Foundation *Before the Fall* (October 2025) [priority: Week 2]
- [ ] IFS *Living Standards, Poverty and Inequality in the UK 2024* — Cribb, Joyce, Waters [priority: Week 2]
- [ ] Wealth Tax Commission Final Report (December 2020, wealthandpolicy.com) + executive summaries of 50+ evidence papers [priority: Weeks 7-9]
- [ ] IFS Deaton Review of Inequalities (~30 evidence papers + 2025 book *Challenging Inequalities*) [priority: ongoing reference]

### Papers
- [ ] Advani, Hughson & Summers (2023) "How much tax do the rich really pay?" *Oxford Review of Economic Policy* [priority: Week 8]
- [ ] Advani, Burgherr & Summers — non-dom migration elasticity papers (CAGE 2022; *EJ* 2025) [priority: Week 8]
- [ ] Piketty & Saez (2003) "Income Inequality in the US 1913-1998" *QJE* — foundational top-incomes paper [priority: Week 16]
- [ ] OECD (2018) *Role and Design of Net Wealth Taxes* [priority: Week 9]
